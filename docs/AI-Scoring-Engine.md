# AI Scoring Engine

## Vision

ConvoCoach is not an AI that simply generates dating messages.

Its mission is to help users build meaningful conversations and healthier relationships through transparent, evidence-backed communication intelligence.

Every insight generated by the system must satisfy four principles:

1. Evidence before conclusion.
2. Confidence before certainty.
3. Explain every recommendation.
4. Never manipulate users or fabricate signals.

- Evidence
- Recommendation

7. Red Flags
Document what signals may indicate caution.
For example:
Consistently one-word replies
Never asks questions
Repeatedly ignores topics
Long unexplained response delays (without overinterpreting)
Frequent conversation shutdowns
Important: Treat these as signals, not proof. Avoid declaring someone a "red flag" as a fact.

The AI must never:

- Pretend to know someone's true feelings.
- Claim certainty about attraction.
- Encourage manipulation or deception.
- Encourage harassment or pressure.
- Overstate conclusions from limited data.

The AI should instead communicate uncertainty clearly and frame outputs as observations and likelihoods rather than facts.
